About merritt k

merritt k (formerly known as Merritt Kopas) is a former game designer, writer, podcaster, digital magazine editor, and wonderful Twitter-er. As a transwoman, k has been a model for women, trans, and non-binary people in the tech world. She has also pushed for greater racial diversity; to end stigma surrounding sexuality, kink, and sex work; and for the full inclusion of all in tech spheres. Beginning with advocacy in Twine games by creating games herself and compiling the book Video Games for Humans.

Since "breaking up" with video games, k has focused on more traditional writing using primarily online platforms. Currently, she is a managing editor for Fanbyte where she continues to push for the inclusion of all in the many realms of fandom.
